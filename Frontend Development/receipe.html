Santosh's receipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 3/4 Cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 Teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 Cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup of tea
  • 1 Cup butter
  • 3 Cup bread flour

For the filling

  • 2/3 Cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 table spoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 Cup butter

Instuctions

  1. Mix the milk with yeast sugar
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined in a dough
  4. Kned the dough for 10 minitues
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap
  6. Ready to serve the bread
